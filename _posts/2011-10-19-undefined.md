title: Double Whammy - Filer and FLAC Player Updates

[Filer 2.1.5](/filer) and [FLAC Player 1.2.1](/flacplayer) have both been approved and released today.

[Filer 2.1.5](http://itunes.apple.com/WebObjects/MZStore.woa/wa/viewSoftware?id=318884764) brings fixes for iOS 5 as well as full-screen file viewing and web browsing on iPad in landscape mode.

[FLAC Player 1.2.1](http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/flac-player/id390532592?ls=1&amp;mt=8) brings a couple of bug fixes to the recent 1.2.0 release.
